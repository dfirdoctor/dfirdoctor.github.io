## Password cracking
One of my interest is password cracking. This is a elegant and analytical kind of sport. Once you begun then you'l never quit.

I use a variety of tools, mostly
- Hashcat
- Hashtopolis 
- Passware
- Elcomsoft Distributed Password Recovery
